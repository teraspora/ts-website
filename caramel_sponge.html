How to make Banana Caramel Sponge Pudding

Background

John's Banana Caramel Sponge Pudding Recipe

General principle

This pudding consistes of a sponge cake plus a hot caramel sauce to be poured over it.

Once you are comfortable with this method, you will get a feel for it, your understanding and confidence will grow, and you can then experiment!

Making the Sponge

  • Cream together 160g Muscovado sugar with 60g butter.
  • Using an electric whisk, whisk in 4 eggs.
  • Then whisk in 200g self-raising flour.
  • Add about 2 tsp vanilla essence.
  • Then mix in 3 mashed bananas.
  • Pour into a buttered flan dish and bake for around an hour at Gas Mark 4 in the middle of the oven.
  • Test with a thin needle for doneness.

Making the sauce

  • In a heavy cast iron pan, cook together 2n grams Muscovado sugar with n grams of butter and a pinch of salt.

To serve

  • Let the sponge cool, cut a slice, then pour the hot sauce over it.
  • If desired, top with a dollop of clotted cream (made by whisking up some double cream).
