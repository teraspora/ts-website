Shortbread - Catherine's Recipe

Ingredients

  • 210g self-raising flour
  • 140g butter
  • 70g sugar (50g muscovado + 20g white sugar)

Method

  • Rub the butter into the flour and sugar. Mix all ingredients to a crumbly dough.
  • Wrap, and chill in the fridge for 20 minutes or so.
  • Meanwhile, preheat the oven to Gas Mark 5-ish (190°C / 374°F)
  • Digitally press the dough into a butter-greased flan dish. Roughen the top with a fork.
  • Bake for about 20 minutes until the top begins to brown and it smells really good!
  • Score into 8 or 12 sectors and let cool before serving with whipped cream or custard.
